About Me

Hello! My name is Bhargavi Gundla. I am a fourth year student at the Georgia Institute of Technology, studying Media and Interactive Design. This site has been created to present a culmination of my personal and professional interests.

Outside of school, I spend my time working on my various artistic interests. Presently, I have been focusing on embroidery, photography and making clothing sustainably with secondhand materials. This website is a work in progress as I upload these creative projects, but in the meantime, please feel free to explore my resume and uploaded creative work!

My Education

Georgia Institute of Technology

B.S Computational Media, Class of 2023, 3.5 GPA

I transferred to Georgia Tech in summer of 2021. I am currently pursuing a degree in Computational Media, specializing in the Media and Interactive Design threads. This program, unique to Georgia Tech's curriculum, provides students with a dual foundation of visual design and computer science. Through my coursework, I have developed an understanding of the technical and analytical perspectives of game development and Web UI/UX.

My expected graduation is December 2023.

University of Georgia, Honors College

B.S Computer Science, Minor Studio Art, 3.8 GPA

I pursued a bachelors in Computer Science, and a Minor in Studio Art during my time at UGA. While I enjoyed my time at UGA, I wanted to find a computer science program that catered to my design-centric interests. Thus, I transferred to Georgia Tech after my second year of study.
